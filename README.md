# healthy_food_market_analysis
data driven analysis to recemmend  a healthy food product strategy


##  Problem Statement
A client wanted to launch a healthy food product but was unsure about product type, pricing, and customer preferences.

##  Approach
- Collected data from 60+ respondents
- Cleaned and analyzed data using Python and Excel
- Identified trends in product demand, pricing, and behavior

## Key Insights
- Salads were the most preferred product
- ₹100–200 is the ideal price range
- Taste is the most important factor
- Poor taste perception is the biggest barrier

##  Recommendation
Launch a mid-priced healthy meal product (salads) focusing on taste and nutrition, distributed through local stores.
